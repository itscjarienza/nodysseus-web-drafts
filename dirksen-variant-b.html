About the Firm

Over 30 Years
Engineering
Southwest Texas

Kenneth R. Dirksen, P.E., R.P.L.S. began his career with Chicago Bridge and Iron in 1989 and joined TxDOT in 1997 — bringing municipal-grade expertise to every project.

After years supervising TxDOT design and construction in Uvalde, Ken founded Dirksen Engineering to serve as an independent consultant focused on land development and municipal design.

Staff includes experienced field crew and office technicians skilled in working with clients, title companies, leading institutions, and local government officers to meet every client's needs.
